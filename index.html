About

Operations experience, engineered into automation

Ahmed has built his career in hospitality operations — with a strong focus on front office and guest-facing service. He understands the real challenges hotels face every day: guest inquiries, complaints, follow-ups, internal coordination, reporting, service recovery, and the communication gaps that slow teams down.

His current focus is building AI and automation solutions that support hotel teams, improve response time, reduce repetitive work, and enhance the guest journey. The goal is practical, not theoretical: systems that fit how hotels actually operate and free staff to spend more time on the guest.

I help hotels improve guest experience, reduce manual work, improve response time, automate internal workflows, and create added value through AI agents, n8n automation, voice assistants, reporting dashboards, and hospitality-focused systems.
